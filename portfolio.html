How does the concept of chatbot arise?

• In 1950, Alan Turing had published a research paper which describes the possibility of creating machines that could think. This concept made the researches to work towards the Artificial Intelligence.

• As the evolution of this idea, in 1951, Game AI was designed for checkers program. It was the first program which was designed for the game of chess.

• Later after this invention, the birth of Artificial Intelligence was erupted. The term Artificial Intelligence was first coined by John Mc Carthy in 1956.

• The first AI laboratory was established in the year 1959.

• The evolution of this laboratory leads to the invention. The General Motors Robot was designed in 1960.

• The first chatbot ELIZA was created by Joseph Weizenbaum in the year 1961 to stimulate conversation between machines and humans. It uses pattern matching and substitution methodology to mimic human conversation.

• IBM Deep Blue game of chess was developed in 1997 in which AI beats the world champion. In the history, Deep Blue was the first computer to beat the world champion in both a chess game and a chess match under regular time controls.

• In 2005, the DARPA (Defense Advanced Research Project Agency) Grand Challenge in the domain of Autonomous Robotic Car was conducted.

IBM Watson (i.e.) IBM’s Question answering system was developed in the year 2011 which is the AI-powered virtual agent to feed the need of the customers across any messaging platform, application, device or channel for customer conversations

The tasks include human intelligence such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making and translates between languages. Chatbot is used in Google predictive Search Engines using the concept of DL and ML. Machine Learning involves building a predictive model that can be used to find a solution for the given problem statement. It works on by defining an object, gathering the data about the object, preparing and working on those data, exploring the data also called as EDA (Exploring Data Analysis), Building a model, model evaluation, finally predicting the outcomes. SIRI, Google Now and Alexa uses both text and voice based chatbots. Chatbots are designed using the following two approaches. CHATBOT FOR MAKING RESERVATIONS AND BOOKINGS:

We have seen that chatbot is been used in almost all the fields of automation and real-world applications. Have you ever wondered can chatbot reserve a seat for the customer in the resorts by the phenomenon of automation? At times, human might even be confused that have they talked to a bot or a human!!! The chatbot is the interface between the customers and the owner. To implement this method, firstly, install a chatbot on the website This will be helpful to connect with the customers and to guide them in a simplified manner through the steps of the reservation using a bot. It is a fun method to reserve the seats with the chatbot. The seats can also be booked through the voice based chatbot in which the customer imitates the bot to book a seat in so and so resort. The bot further stores this data given by the customer as the input and analyze the resort data and look for available seat in the resort. If the seat is available, it itself books the seat for the customer. Finally, the chatbot reminds the customer that it has booked a seat in the so-called resort and inform him about the details. In this, the chatbot eliminates the stress of booking seats for the customer and the stress of checking the availability of the seats for the resort authorities on the other side. Reservation chatbots provides adequate amount of the personalized experience than booking websites. The mind-blowing work is that Booking.com and Skyscanner has already created similar bots to do this work. Google has also created a demo bot which reserves the seats in the resorts and acts as the intermediate between the customer and the clients. Rather than clicking on the screen and booking seats, this type of reservation provides more natural experience if speaking with the customer. The chatbots responds to the real-world environment about the services and conditions of service, give recommendations, receive complaints and try to resolve it by the code given by the programmer and many more. This leaves a land mark in the technology to use bots in the real time environment in order to focus on the advanced mode of development and reduce the workload on the human through the efforts of the chatbot.
